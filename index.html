What We're Digging Into at the Bunker:

Our mission is to develop and support a diverse range of unique skirmish game settings, each designed to ignite your imagination and challenge your tactical acumen. While our armory is always expanding, here's a glimpse of the battlegrounds you can soon explore:

Gamma Zone: Brave the irradiated, anomaly-strewn horrors of a post-cataclysmic exclusion zone. Lead a small squad of desperate survivors, cunning mercenaries, or fanatical faction members as you scavenge for reality-bending artifacts, battle grotesque mutants, and navigate the treacherous politics of a world gone mad. With its gritty atmosphere, dark humor, and focus on high-stakes survival, Gamma Zone offers a deeply immersive and challenging experience.

Cowpoke: Saddle up, partner! Dust off your Stetson and load your six-shooter for fast-paced, cinematic showdowns in the rugged landscapes of the Wild West. From tense duels at high noon to desperate saloon brawls and daring train robberies, Cowpoke will let you forge your legend as a stoic lawman, a notorious outlaw, a determined bounty hunter, or any other iconic figure of the untamed frontier.

Viking Brawl: Hear the clash of steel and the roar of the shield wall! Viking Brawl hurls you into the brutal, intimate combat of the Viking Age. Lead your warband of fierce Norse warriors, stoic Saxons, or other historical combatants in raids, skirmishes, and desperate defenses where honor is earned in blood and glory awaits the brave in Valhalla. Skalds will sing of your victories... or your noble defeats!

Why 28mm Skirmish?

We love the 28mm scale for its perfect balance of detail and practicality. Miniatures at this scale have real character, allowing for impressive painting and customization. Skirmish games, focusing on individual models or small squads, emphasize tactical choices, resource management, and narrative development over massed ranks. Every loss is felt, every victory hard-earned. It's personal, it's intense, and it's incredibly rewarding.
