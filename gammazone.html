DARE YOU ENTER THE GAMMA ZONE?

Survival is an Option. Sanity is Not Guaranteed.

Hear the Geiger counter's insistent click? Feel that prickle on your skin as unseen energies warp the very air around you? That's the Gamma Zone beckoning – an irradiated, anomaly-twisted heart of darkness where only the desperate, the daring, or the damned come seeking fortune, forbidden knowledge, or just one more precious day of survival.

Gamma Zone is a 28mm tabletop skirmish wargame that thrusts you into this unforgiving post-cataclysmic exclusion zone. You command a small, hand-picked squad of fighters – perhaps grim Zone Regulation Authority troopers, curious Aurora Foundation scientists, pragmatic Iron Sledge mercenaries, ruthless Syndicate Cartel enforcers, freedom-loving Wayfinders, or just rugged Independents trying to carve out an existence. Your mission: navigate treacherous landscapes, scavenge vital supplies and reality-bending artifacts, battle grotesque mutants and equally dangerous rival stalkers, and confront the Zone’s myriad inexplicable horrors.

What Awaits You in the Irradiated Depths:

A Living, Killing Environment: The Zone itself is your greatest adversary. Face physics-defying anomalies that can crush, burn, or transmute unfortunate souls. Endure sudden emission surges that force you to scramble for shelter. Every step is a gamble, every shadow a potential threat.

Tense, Tactical Skirmishes: With an intuitive 2D6 roll-under system, every action is fraught with consequence. Careful positioning, judicious use of cover (from flimsy barricades to solid ruins), and smart activation choices are paramount. Will you risk an aimed shot, lay down suppressing fire, or make a desperate charge?

Your Squad, Your Story: Lead a unique crew of fighters, each with their own stats and (in scenarios) pre-defined gear reflecting their faction's doctrine. The (optional) Advanced Rules will allow for full squad customization, faction benefits, and campaign progression where your survivors gain experience, scars, and notoriety.

Dark Humor & Gritty Survival: The Zone is a grim place, but it’s not without its moments of bleak comedy. Expect a game where plans go spectacularly awry, critical failures lead to hilarious catastrophes, and hard-won victories feel truly earned against impossible odds.

The Zone is Not for the Faint of Heart:

Gamma Zone isn't just about who has the biggest gun (though it helps!). It's about outthinking your opponents, outlasting the environment, and making those gut-wrenching choices when your supplies are low and something inhuman is howling in the distance. Prepare for moments of sheer terror, unexpected triumph, and the uniquely satisfying crunch of a successful mission in a world that actively wants you dead.

The full Gamma Zone rulebook is materializing from the Zone's depths, packed with core rules, distinct factions, engaging scenarios for pre-generated squads, and a clear path to advanced customization and campaign play.

Prepare your miniatures. Check your dice. The Gamma Zone is waiting.
